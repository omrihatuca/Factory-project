In that page you have 5 option to choose, every choise will take you to another page, the option is:

  • Department page- if you want to see all the information about department section and do actions.
  • Employee page- if you want to see all the information about employee section and do actions.
  • Shift page- if you want to see all the information about shift section and do actions.
  • Shift information page- if you want to see only information about shifts without employees.
  • Log-in page- if you want to startover.
